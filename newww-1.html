✦ My Story

Art rooted in tradition, bloomed by passion

From the narrow lanes of Unn Patiya, Surat, Sana has been adorning hands since childhood — learning the sacred art of mehndi from her grandmother, who believed every swirl carries a blessing.

Her designs are a dialogue between Arabic geometry, Rajasthani florals, and modern fusion — each piece hand-crafted with natural henna paste, leaving a deep, long-lasting stain and an unforgettable impression.

✦ How it works

From consultation
to lasting colour

01

Design Consultation

Share your vision, event type, and inspiration. Together we choose the perfect style and coverage for your occasion.

02

Natural Paste Prep

Fresh henna paste made with natural ingredients — no chemicals. Prepared fresh for each client for the deepest, richest stain.

03

The Application

Sana draws freehand, cone in hand, turning your skin into a canvas. Each stroke placed with intention and love.

04

Aftercare & Bloom

Leave the paste on for 6–8 hours. Rub with a sugar-lemon glaze. Watch your mehndi deepen to a rich mahogany over 48 hours.
